Discover Turkey

Where East meets West in a tapestry of cultures

Crossroads of Civilizations

Turkey offers a captivating blend of Eastern and Western influences, with a rich history spanning ancient civilizations, Byzantine splendor, and Ottoman grandeur. From the vibrant streets of Istanbul to the otherworldly landscapes of Cappadocia, Turkey promises an unforgettable journey through time and culture.

Top Destinations in Turkey

Istanbul

Explore this transcontinental city where Europe meets Asia, home to iconic landmarks like the Hagia Sophia, Blue Mosque, and Grand Bazaar.

Pamukkale

Marvel at the "Cotton Castle" with its stunning white terraces of mineral-rich thermal waters and ancient Hierapolis ruins.

Cappadocia

Experience the surreal lunar landscape with its fairy chimneys, cave dwellings, and the magical sight of hundreds of hot air balloons at dawn.

Turkish Cuisine

Turkish cuisine reflects the country's rich cultural heritage, with influences from the Mediterranean, Middle East, Central Asia, and the Balkans. From savory kebabs and mezes to sweet baklava and Turkish delight, the culinary journey is as diverse as it is delightful.

Best Time to Visit

Spring (April to May) and fall (September to October) offer the most pleasant weather for exploring Turkey. Summer (June to August) is peak tourist season with hot temperatures, while coastal areas remain mild in winter (November to March).

Travel Tips

  • Currency: Turkish Lira (₺)
  • Language: Turkish
  • Transport: Extensive bus network, domestic flights between major cities
  • Power: Type C and F sockets, 220V
  • Internet: Widely available in urban areas, local SIM cards recommended
  • Payment: Cash commonly used, cards accepted in tourist areas
