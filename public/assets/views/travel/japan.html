Discover Japan

Where ancient traditions meet futuristic innovations

Land of the Rising Sun

Japan is a fascinating blend of ancient traditions and cutting-edge technology, where serene temples stand in the shadows of soaring skyscrapers. From the bustling metropolis of Tokyo to the historic streets of Kyoto, Japan offers visitors an unparalleled cultural experience.

Top Destinations in Japan

Tokyo

Experience the world's most populous metropolis, where historic temples and gardens coexist with futuristic technology and architecture.

Kyoto

Discover over 1,600 Buddhist temples, 400 Shinto shrines, and the historic wooden architecture of Japan's cultural capital.

Mount Fuji

Climb Japan's highest and most iconic mountain, a sacred site and enduring symbol of Japanese culture.

Japanese Cuisine

Japanese cuisine is renowned worldwide for its emphasis on fresh, seasonal ingredients and meticulous preparation. From delicate sushi and hearty ramen to savory okonomiyaki and sweet mochi, the culinary landscape is as diverse as it is delicious.

Best Time to Visit

The spring (March to May) and fall (September to November) seasons offer the most pleasant weather and spectacular natural scenery. Cherry blossom season in late March to early April and autumn foliage in November are particularly popular times to visit.

Travel Tips

  • Currency: Japanese Yen (¥)
  • Language: Japanese
  • Transport: Efficient public transport with the JR Rail Pass recommended for tourists
  • Power: Type A and B sockets, 100V
  • Internet: Widely available, pocket WiFi rental recommended
  • Payment: Still cash-heavy, but cards increasingly accepted
